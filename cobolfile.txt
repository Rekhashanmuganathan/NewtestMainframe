
       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. COPYARRAY.

       ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
       INPUT-OUTPUT SECTION.
       FILE-CONTROL.
           SELECT STUDENT-FILE ASSIGN TO 'students.csv'
               ORGANIZATION IS LINE SEQUENTIAL.

       DATA DIVISION.
       FILE SECTION.
       FD  STUDENT-FILE.
       01  STUDENT-REC       PIC X(200).

       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01  WS-COUNT          PIC 9(3) VALUE 3.
       01  CNT               PIC 9(3).
       01  STUDENT-TABLE.
           05  STUDENT-ENTRY OCCURS 100 TIMES.
               10 T-ID       PIC X(5).
               10 T-NAME     PIC X(30).
               10 T-AGE      PIC X(3).
               10 T-GRADE    PIC X(2).

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
       MAIN.
           *> Populate table (example)
           MOVE "00001"       TO T-ID(1)
           MOVE "Alice Johnson" TO T-NAME(1)
           MOVE "20"          TO T-AGE(1)
           MOVE "A+"          TO T-GRADE(1)

           MOVE "00002"       TO T-ID(2)
           MOVE "Bob Smith"    TO T-NAME(2)
           MOVE "21"          TO T-AGE(2)
           MOVE "B"           TO T-GRADE(2)

           MOVE "00003"       TO T-ID(3)
           MOVE "Carol Lee"    TO T-NAME(3)
           MOVE "19"          TO T-AGE(3)
           MOVE "A"           TO T-GRADE(3)

           OPEN OUTPUT STUDENT-FILE

           PERFORM VARYING CNT FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL CNT > WS-COUNT
               STRING T-ID(CNT) DELIMITED BY SIZE ","
                      T-NAME(CNT) DELIMITED BY SIZE ","
                      T-AGE(CNT) DELIMITED BY SIZE ","
                      T-GRADE(CNT) DELIMITED BY SIZE
                   INTO STUDENT-REC
               WRITE STUDENT-REC
           END-PERFORM

           CLOSE STUDENT-FILE

           Display "Data written to students.csv successfully."
           Display "Program completed."
           
           STOP RUN.
